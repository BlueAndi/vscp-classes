**Default unit:** watt per square metre per nm (W·m-2·nm-1)   
**Opt. unit:** watt per metre3 (W·m-3) (1), watt per square metre per hertz (W·m-2·Hz-1) (2)

Irradiance of a surface per unit frequency or wavelength.
